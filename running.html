Beer Half Marathon on Sunday 7th November 2021. The plan is to do roughly a 5k (four times) starting on the hour and then get a drink (optional, but recommended).
  • [Start at 2:00 pm] Chamberlain Square to The Plough, Harborne (route) followed by a drink.
  • [Start at 3:00 pm] The Plough, Harborne to Attic Brewery, Stirchley (route) followed by a drink.
  • [Start at 4:00 pm] Attic Brewery, Stirchley to The Old Moseley Arms (route) followed by a drink.

RUNNING GROUPS IN BIRMINGHAM

Running groups (free):

  • Run Brum Crew (Instagram, Twitter) on Mondays at 18:30 from Victoria Square (location). Register at https://groups.runtogether.co.uk/RunBrumCrew

  • District Run Run Crew (Instagram) on Tuesdays at 18:15 from BrewDog (location). No registration necessary.

  • Green Heart Runners (Instagram, Twitter) on Tuesdays at 17:30 from outside the Main Library on the University of Birmingham campus (location). Register at https://groups.runtogether.co.uk/GreenHeartRunners/Runs

  • City of Birmingham Striders (Instagram) on Thursdays at 18:30 from St. Paul's Square (location). Register at https://groups.runtogether.co.uk/COBS/runs

  • ParkRun (https://www.parkrun.org.uk/edgbastonreservoir) on Saturdays 09:00 at Edgbaston Park Reservoir (location).

  • ParkRun (https://www.parkrun.org.uk/cannonhill/) on Saturdays 09:00 at Cannon Hill Park (location).

  • Run Beer Social (Instagram, Facebook) from The Wolf (location). No registration necessary. No fixed schedule, but the runs are advertised well in advance on their socials.

Running tours (paid):

  • Run of a Kind (https://www.runofakindbirmingham.com). They offer a few different running tours based such as Best of Birmingham, Digbeth Street Art, Leafy Brum, etc. Ideal if you're new to Birmingham or have someone visiting you, but there is also lots new to learn even if you're a born and bred Brummie. Check out more on their Instagram page (https://www.instagram.com/runofakindbirmingham)
