Beer Half Marathon on Sunday 7th November 2021

The plan is to do roughly a 5k (four times) starting on the hour and then get a drink (optional, but recommended).
Participation is at your own risk!
  • [Start at 2:00 pm] Chamberlain Square to The Plough, Harborne (route) followed by a drink.
  • [Start at 3:00 pm] The Plough, Harborne to Attic Brewery, Stirchley (route) followed by a drink.
  • [Start at 4:00 pm] Attic Brewery, Stirchley to The Old Moseley Arms (route) followed by a drink.
  • [Start at 5:00 pm] The Old Moseley Arms to Chamberlain Square (route) followed by a drink at Purecraft.
Attic Brewery is close to Bournville train station if you want to join for part of the run.
